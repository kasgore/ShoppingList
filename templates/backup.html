Restore from backup

Replaces all recipes, ingredients, lists, meal plans, and photos with the contents of an uploaded backup. Your current state is snapshotted first (see below) so a bad restore is reversible.

Automatic backups

{% if backup_enabled %}

A full backup ZIP (DB + photos) is written to {{ auto_backup_dir }} nightly at the configured hour (default 3 am). The 7 newest are kept on disk; older ones rotate out automatically.

Tip: mount that directory as a network share or rclone-sync it to Backblaze B2 / Google Drive to get truly off-site copies. Right now it lives on the Pi's volume — fine for "I broke the DB" but not for "the Pi is gone."
